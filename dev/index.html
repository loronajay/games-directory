01 // FOUNDER VISION
Build a modern arcade platform with classic competition.
Jay’s Retro Arcade is designed as a platform rather than a single release. The goal is to create a recognizable arcade ecosystem built around quick-play original games, competitive replay loops, and strong community identity. The web arcade is the first step toward a larger system that can eventually exist both online and in physical arcade spaces.
