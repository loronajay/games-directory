A browser-based arcade platform that delivers fast, competitive retro-style games with console-like presentation, designed to run both online and inside physical arcade cabinets.

2. The Core Vision

Why the project exists.

Vision

Modern gaming has become dominated by long-form, high-commitment experiences. Jay’s Retro Arcade brings back the spirit of classic arcades — quick to start, easy to understand, difficult to master, and built around competition.

The goal is to create a modern arcade ecosystem that exists both:

online as a playable web platform

physically through custom arcade cabinets

Players can jump into short, high-energy games and compete for scores, rankings, and community recognition.

3. The Problem

Investors and supporters need this clearly stated.

The Problem

Modern gaming has several barriers that classic arcades did not:

Long installation times

Large storage requirements

High learning curves

Time commitment

Lack of quick competitive experiences

Meanwhile, traditional arcades have declined due to:

expensive hardware

maintenance complexity

limited game variety

difficulty updating machines

There is currently no modern platform that combines:

instant access gaming

arcade-style competition

flexible hardware deployment

web-scale distribution

4. The Solution

This is where your platform fits.

The Solution

Jay’s Retro Arcade is designed as a hybrid arcade platform.

It combines the accessibility of web games with the presentation and reliability of a physical arcade machine.

The platform consists of three interconnected layers:

Cloud Arcade Platform
A web-based arcade accessible instantly from any browser.

Offline Arcade OS
A local version of the platform that runs inside arcade cabinets and automatically syncs with the cloud when internet is available.

Arcade Hardware Ecosystem
Physical cabinets powered by the platform, enabling deployment in homes, events, and commercial venues.
