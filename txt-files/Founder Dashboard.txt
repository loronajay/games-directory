Core Vision Reminder

Jay’s Retro Arcade is a modern arcade ecosystem that combines:

• original arcade-style games
• a web-based arcade operating system
• arcade cabinet hardware
• competitive leaderboards
• tournaments and community competition

Players can experience the platform through:

• web browsers
• mobile devices
• gamepads
• arcade cabinets

The long-term goal is a global competitive arcade network.
