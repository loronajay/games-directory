But the truth about Kickstarter is this:

Most campaigns fail.

Not because the idea is bad.

Because people misunderstand how Kickstarter works.

A successful Kickstarter is usually 70% marketing preparation and 30% campaign.

The 5 Things That Make Kickstarters Succeed
1 — Clear Product

People must understand it in 5 seconds.

Example:

A modern retro arcade cabinet you can play at home.

Simple.

2 — Great Visuals

Kickstarter is visual.

You need:

• gameplay clips
• cabinet renders
• UI previews
• short videos

3 — Emotional Hook

People back stories.

Not products.

Example:

I'm building the arcade machine I always wanted as a kid.

4 — Community Before Launch

Most successful campaigns already have:

• Discord
• email list
• social followers

before launch.

5 — Strong Rewards

Kickstarter isn't just donations.

People need good tiers.

Example:

Tier	Reward
$5	supporter
$25	name in credits
$60	early game pack
$399	early cabinet
$599	deluxe cabinet
